I can’t open my form in Portable Document Format (PDF). What can I do?

Class II nuclear facilities and prescribed equipment ACR form

  • Nuclear substances and radiation devices licence ACR form

These forms may be updated from time-to-time, so please ensure that you always use the most current version available form on the web site.

Open the ACR document then select the applicable form from the drop-down list and click the “Generate Form” btn to apply your selection. The ACR forms are customized for the activity authorized by the licence. The licensed activity name and number (e.g. 812 - industrial radiography), can be found on each licence under section IV Licensed Activities.

If you selected the wrong licensed activity, in order to change it, go back to the drop-down list then select the correct licensed activity and then click on the “Generate Form” btn to create the correct ACR form.

To request a hard copy of the ACR form, please send your request to dnsr-info-drsn@cnsc-ccsn.gc.ca.

Information:

Some information requested may be submitted as a separate spreadsheet attached to the same email as the form. Please ensure your spreadsheet uses the same headings as the referenced table and contains all required information.
