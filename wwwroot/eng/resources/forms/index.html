The Canadian Nuclear Safety Commission (CNSC) regulates all aspects of nuclear energy and materials, ensuring that strict licensing and certification rules are followed for the possession, use, packaging, transport, storage, and import and export of nuclear substances in order to protect the health, safety and security of Canadians and the environment.
