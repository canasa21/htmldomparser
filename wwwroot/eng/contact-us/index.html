Privacy Act Statement

The Canadian Nuclear Safety Commission (CNSC) is committed to providing visitors with a website that respects their privacy. Any personal information collected by the CNSC is collected under the authority of the Communications Policy of the Government of Canada and section 7 of the Financial Administration Act. Some personal information is required if you wish the CNSC to contact you about your inquiry and/or comments. All personal information is protected under the Privacy Act and disposed of in accordance with legislative requirements. Personal information is described in Standard PIB PSU 904 (Automated Document, Records, Information Management Systems) in the Government of Canada's Info Source Publications.
