Who are we?

We are highly skilled nuclear professionals who are committed to safeguarding the health, safety and security of our fellow Canadians and our environment.

Joining our team means you will have an opportunity to make a meaningful impact and help keep Canadians safe! Our diverse staff is made up of scientists and engineers, corporate professionals, new graduates, students and alumni.
