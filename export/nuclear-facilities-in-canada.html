Nuclear licensees operate all across Canada. They include uranium mines and mills, uranium processing facilities, nuclear power plants, research reactors, particle accelerators, and nuclear substances processing facilities. All of the following facilities are licensed by the CNSC and are subject to strict regulatory oversight to ensure the safety of the public and the environment.
