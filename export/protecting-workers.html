    The CNSC licenses dosimetry services in order to ensure accurate and precise dose measurements. Dosimetry service licensees are obligated to regularly file information with Health Canada's National Dose Registry (NDR).

    The NDR, operated by Health Canada's Radiation Protection Bureau, includes radiation dose records from all commercial dosimetry processors, nuclear power generating facilities, Canadian Nuclear Laboratories Ltd., and uranium mines. The NDR also includes dose records from X-ray users such as dentists, radiologists, chiropractors as well as INFO-0827: Introduction to Dosimetry (PDF)
